History of Falafels

Falafel is a delicious Middle Eastern dish with a rich history. It's believed to have originated in Egypt over a thousand years ago, made from ground chickpeas or fava beans.

It quickly gained popularity throughout the Middle East and eventually made its way to the rest of the world.
